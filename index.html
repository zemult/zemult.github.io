About Surreal

Surreal is a multipurpose Discord bot. You can play games with it or utilize it for education.

What you should use our bot

Surreal surpasses all the other multipurpose Discord bots because it doesn't only focus on Discord related purposes, it also focuses on educational purposes. By that, we mean that you can use this bot to hold debates on a topic via a specific command, you can use it to quiz yourself on a vast range of mathematics, programming, and more, and lastly you can use this bot to learn how to program using the Discord.py library.
